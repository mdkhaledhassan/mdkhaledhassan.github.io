Shopking - Customer App

Description

  • Shopping

  • Playstore

  • Jan 10, 2024

Introducing ShopKing, an innovative eCommerce application and business management solution crafted to elevate the performance of single or multi-branch retail businesses. ShopKing offers a comprehensive suite of tools, including a dynamic Point of Sale (POS) system and intuitive flutter mobile application for users, coupled with robust inventory management. At the heart of ShopKing is its user-centric design, featuring a customer app for seamless online shopping experiences, a user-friendly website for browsing and ordering.

Technology

  • Dart
  • Flutter
